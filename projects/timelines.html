Clinical Trial Timeline

A validated critical-path (CPM) engine that turns a study's visit schedule into an editable timeline and milestone rail.

Launch live demo Runs in your browser

What it does

Computes the full study schedule server-side from study parameters and renders it as an on-brand, inline-editable spreadsheet plus milestone rail.

How it works

  • A Python CPM engine is the single source of truth for the schedule.
  • The timeline renders as an editable spreadsheet with an xlsx round-trip.
  • This single-file prototype is the design/behavior reference for the productionized app.
