Pharmacokinetic Analysis NCA

Recreation of the pharmacokinetic statistics workflow: a statistical analysis plan, bioanalytical-to-EDC sample reconciliation, non-compartmental analysis (NCA), and the standard PK result figures. The concentration-time data is simulated from a 1-compartment oral model (with log-normal between-subject variability), then run through a real NCA engine in the browser, linear-up / log-down trapezoidal AUC, terminal-slope lambda-z, BLQ rules, so every parameter, table, and figure is computed, not hard-coded. Synthetic study.
